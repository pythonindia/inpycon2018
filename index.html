SPEAKERS

Armin Ronacher

Armin Ronacher is the creator of the Flask Web Framework and principal software engineer at sentry.io. He is leading an engineering team in Vienna Austria working on Sentry and a frequesnt speaker at tech and open source conferences.

Armin Ronacher

Creator of Flask
Software Engineer, Sentry.io

Carol Willing

Carol Willing is a Python Software Foundation Fellow and former Director, a core developer of CPython and Project Jupyter, and a Research Software Engineer at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo. She's also Geek-In-Residence at Fab Lab San Diego and co-organizes Pyladies San Diego and San Diego Python.

Carol Willing

Former Director, PSF
Core Developer, CPython & Project Jupyter

Travis E. Oliphant

Travis E. Oliphant is a Founder of Quansight and Director at Anaconda Inc. He co-founded Anaconda Inc and he has worked with Python for numerical and scientific programming, notably as the primary developer of the NumPy and as a founding contributor of the SciPy. He actively contributes to s/w development and engages with the wider open source community in Python ecosystem.

Travis E. Oliphant

Founder, Quansight
Director, Anaconda Inc.
